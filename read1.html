A doctor in the US Paediatrician Steen James: the best sport for young children is swimming

Dr Steen James, a paediatrician at Pediatrics Village Medical Centre in the US, said that the best sport for young children is swimming. According to the doctor, swimming helps improve flexibility and coordination, as well as has a good effect on the psyche. In addition, this sport can protect a child from accidental drowning, which becomes especially important in summer.

"swimming is not just fun and fitness,
but an important life skill that can save a child's life"

- noted American doctor Steen James
