      <h1 id="read-h1">Water Behaviour Rules</h1>
      
      <p id="read-p">TIPS FOR SWIMMING</p>
      <ul>
        <li>swim only in authorised places;</li>
        <li>do not dive in unfamiliar places - there may be submerged logs, stones, snags, etc. at the bottom;</li>
        <li>do not swim far from the shore on inflatable watercrafts - they may be defective, which is very dangerous even for those who can swim well;</li>
        <li>do not swim in stormy weather or in high surf;</li>
        <li>You should start swimming at a water temperature of at least 18 degrees Celsius and in clear, windless weather at an air temperature of 25 degrees Celsius or more.</li>
    </ul>

    <p id="read-p">BASIC RULES FOR CHILDREN'S SAFE BEHAVIOUR ON THE WATER</p>
      <ul>
        <li>when swimming, there must be an adult nearby;</li>
        <li>Do not play in places where you can fall into the water;</li>
        <li>don't go into a deep place;</li>
        <li>do not swim beyond the buoys;</li>
    </ul>
